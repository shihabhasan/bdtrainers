Hued Theme is responsive and loaded with customization options. Featured include various layouts, ability to customize color scheme, various fonts, custom background, beautiful author info block, social media links and much more... Change sidebar location to left, right, or hide it completely. It is beautifully designed to showcase all your blog posts and memories. Supports WordPress v4.2 and above.
